Our Experience

Project Lost Temple

The Lost Temple is a cinematic environment I created using Unreal Engine, inspired by ancient ruins hidden deep within forgotten jungles. This project focuses on atmosphere, storytelling through environment design, and the interplay of light and shadow. I used high-resolution textures, dynamic lighting, fog effects, and foliage placement to bring a sense of realism and mystery. The goal was to immerse viewers in a world where nature has reclaimed the remnants of an ancient civilization.

💡 Tools Used: Unreal Engine 5 | Quixel Megascans | Lumen Lighting | Nanite Geometry

📌 Key Features:

  • Custom lighting setup for cinematic mood
  • Dense foliage and environmental storytelling
  • Modular temple assets and realistic weathering
  • Volumetric fog and ambient sound simulation

Project CyCity

CyCity is a futuristic open-world game inspired by the visual tone and atmosphere of cyberpunk-style storytelling. The project is driven by short cinematic shots that showcase a world blending realism with a futuristic edge. I researched to inspiration from modern cities like Tokyo and San Francisco—places that already reflect elements of the future—CyCity combines dense urban environments, neon-drenched streets, and advanced technologies with believable city infrastructure. The core idea behind CyCity is to create a living, breathing world that feels both grounded and visionary. Through deep research into cyberpunk game design and real-world city development, the project aims to merge realistic visuals with futuristic concepts. The environment design reflects current architecture evolved by tech innovation, urban lighting, and immersive atmosphere.

💡 Tools Used: Unreal Engine 5 | Quixel Megascans | Lumen Lighting | Nanite Geometry

📌 Key Features:

  • Custom lighting setup for cinematic mood
  • Dense foliage and environmental storytelling
  • Modular temple assets and realistic weathering
  • Volumetric fog and ambient sound simulation
